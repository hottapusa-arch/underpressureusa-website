About

Built from real experience.

Led by John Hamling, Under Pressure USA is built on 27 years in the underground infrastructure industry, including hot taps, line stops, valve inserts, water systems, and field operations. The goal is simple: show people the systems they rely on every day but rarely see.

Featured Series

Pipe History

Field Knowledge

Real-world lessons from decades of underground utility work.

Infrastructure Education

Making buried systems easier to understand for the public, creators, and industry partners.

Podcast

Under Pressure: After Hours

A more relaxed look at the people, stories, opinions, and humor behind the work. It is blue-collar, honest, and built around the personalities that make the industry what it is.
