Why we do this

Infrastructure deserves better storytelling.

Every day, millions of people rely on systems they never see — water mains, valves, pipelines, treatment facilities, and the crews who keep them operating.

Under Pressure USA exists to bring those stories to the surface through documentary storytelling, real field experience, and conversations with the people who build and maintain critical infrastructure.

We're not here to advertise the industry. We're here to help people understand it.

About the founder

Tell stories worth watching. Start conversations worth having.

Under Pressure USA started with one goal: show people the niche world of hot taps, line stops, and valve inserts.

As the audience grew, it became clear the real story was much bigger than the work itself. Today, Under Pressure USA explores the history, engineering, technology, and people behind the underground infrastructure that keeps our communities running.

Our mission is simple: educate, start conversations, and build relationships that strengthen the industry.
